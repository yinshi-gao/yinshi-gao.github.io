Welcome! I'm Yinshi (Agnes) Gao 高印时

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Supply Chain and Information Systems at Smeal College of Business, Pennsylvania State University. I am fortunately enough to work with Prof. Dan Guide and Prof. Saurabh Bansal. My research primarily focus on incentives in supply chain, servicizing operations and closed-loop supply chain.
